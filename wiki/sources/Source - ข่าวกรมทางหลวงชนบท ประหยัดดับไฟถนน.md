# Source: Rural roads lights-off energy saving debate

## Metadata

## Summary
The source reports public debate around [[Department of Rural Roads]] measures to reduce or switch off road lighting in selected low-risk areas from 1 May 2026.

## Key Points
- Screening criteria include low night traffic, no hazardous points, and no repeated-crash history.
- Field offices should survey, assess, monitor, and restore lights if risk remains.
- Public criticism focuses on safety, crime risk, and the alternative of LED conversion.
