## Summary
Matichon/LINE Today reports criticism by Suphanat Minchaiynunt and others that switching off rural-road lights may reduce safety; the suggested alternative is replacing HPS lighting with LED.

## Key Points
- Criticism argues that pole spacing, height, and placement were designed for needed visibility.
- The source repeats the agency screening criteria for low-risk areas.
- The key proposal is to convert orange/HPS lights to LED instead of removing illumination.
