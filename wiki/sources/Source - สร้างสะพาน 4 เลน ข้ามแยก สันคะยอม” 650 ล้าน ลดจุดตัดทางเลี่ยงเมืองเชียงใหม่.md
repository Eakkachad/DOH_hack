## Summary
Daily News reports draft TOR/procurement details for an interchange project at [[Route 121]] and [[Route 1367]] around San Khayom intersection in Chiang Mai, with a budget around 650 million baht.

## Key Points
- Work limits: Route 121 km 22+350-23+800 and Route 1367 km 2+400-3+800, total about 2.050 km.
- Central price: 649,907,634.78 baht from FY2569 budget of 650 million baht.
- Main structure: twin prestressed-concrete bridge, 4 lanes, about 525 m long, excluding approaches.
- Construction duration: 1,080 days, about 3 years.
