# Source: Four-digit highway selective lights-off measure

## Metadata
- **File:** พ.ค.นี้ ทล. ปิดไฟทางหลวงเลข 4 ตัวบางช่วง 4 ทุ่มถึง 6 โมงเช้า เน้นปลอดภัย ช่วยประหยัดพลังงาน.md

## Summary
The source reports selective lights-off on some four-digit highway sections from May 2026, generally during 22.00-06.00, with stated safety screening.

## Key Points
- The measure is described as affecting about 4% of four-digit highway lighting, from about 500,000 lights.
- Expected energy saving is about 10% for the selected lights-off measure.
- LED replacement is described as a longer-term plan, including about 300,000 lights in 2026 and about 650,000 more in the next year.
