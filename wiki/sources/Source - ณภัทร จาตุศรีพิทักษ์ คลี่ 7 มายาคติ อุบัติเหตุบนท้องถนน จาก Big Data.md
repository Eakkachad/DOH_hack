## Summary
ThaiPublica interviews Dr. Napat Jatusripitak about using Big Data and AI to test road-safety assumptions and evaluate interventions with evidence.

## Key Points
- The article states Thailand had more than 20,000 road deaths per year, or more than 55 per day.
- It discusses multi-agency datasets and policy evaluation for checkpoints, bridge/underpass motorcycle restrictions, and spatial crash risk.
