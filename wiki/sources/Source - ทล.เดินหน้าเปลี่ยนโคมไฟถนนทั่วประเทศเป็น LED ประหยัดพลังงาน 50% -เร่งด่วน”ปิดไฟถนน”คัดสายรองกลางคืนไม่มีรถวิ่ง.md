## Summary
The source reports [[Department of Highways]] plans to convert road lights nationwide to LED while using selective lights-off measures on low-risk secondary/night-low-volume sections.

## Key Points
- LED conversion is described as saving about 50% energy.
- Lights-off screening is limited to sections without safety risk.
- Intersections, U-turns, curves, and other risk points are not intended to be switched off.
