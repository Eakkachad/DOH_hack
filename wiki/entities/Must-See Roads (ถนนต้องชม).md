# Must-See Roads (ถนนต้องชม)

**Must-See Roads** are scenic routes or specific sightseeing points along the highway network managed by the [[Department of Highways]].

## Management
These routes are overseen by the Bureau of Highway Maintenance (สำนักบริหารบำรุงทาง).

## Hackathon Context
In the context of the [[DOH Hackathon 2026]], enhancing the experience of these scenic routes could fall under the **Digital Service & Communication** pillar (e.g., augmented reality tourism guides, smart rest areas) or **Next-Gen Highway Design**.
