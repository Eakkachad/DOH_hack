# DOH HACKATHON 2026: Driving the Future of Highways

The **DOH HACKATHON 2026** is an innovation competition organized by the [[Department of Highways]] and Chulalongkorn University (CUTI). Its goal is to solve pain points in highway management and operations by bridging ideas to prototypes.

## Core Challenges (Themes)
Participants must propose solutions addressing one of these 6 pillars:
1. **Next-Gen Highway & Infrastructure Design**
2. **Smart Construction Management**
3. **Highway Maintenance Innovation**
4. **Highway Safety Solution** (Vision Zero)
5. **Sustainable & Low-Carbon Highway** (Net Zero)
6. **Digital Service & Communication**

## Relevance to Competitors
Since you are competing in this hackathon, your solutions must align with the 5 core judging pillars:
- Problem Identification & Root Cause
- Data-Driven Evidence
- Proposed Solution & Prototype
- Impact, Value & Scalability
- Implementation Roadmap & Realistic Budget
