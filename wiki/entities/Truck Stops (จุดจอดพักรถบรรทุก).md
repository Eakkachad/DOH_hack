# Truck Stops (จุดจอดพักรถบรรทุก)

**Truck Stops** are designated rest areas on highways and motorways specifically for heavy vehicles. 

## Management
The data and locations for these stops are managed by the Office of Vehicle Weight Control (สำนักงานควบคุมน้ำหนักยานพาหนะ). Providing safe and adequate rest areas is crucial for **Highway Safety** and logistics efficiency.

## Hackathon Context
For the [[DOH Hackathon 2026]], these stops can be integrated into the **Highway Safety Solution** or **Digital Service & Communication** pillars (e.g., an app that shows available parking for truckers).
